Wansati - As Flores do Mundo is a documentary, shot in Mozambique, that offers a glimpse into the lives of three seventeen-year-old girls from Maputo: Augusta, Mariana, and Crimilda. Family, school, and rugby are central to their lives. United by a passion for the sport, they challenge gender stereotypes in a society that sees rugby as "only for men." Their stories unfold in the days leading up to Mozambique's first women's rugby tournament, highlighting how rugby empowers them and earns them admiration from their peers.
