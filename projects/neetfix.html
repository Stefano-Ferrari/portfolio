People born between 1997 and 2012 are referred as "Generation Z". This generation have grown up during the economic crysis, is aware of environmental risks for the planet and have difficulties to cope with the pressure of being the last hope for our world. This pressure, usually generate anxiety, mental diseases or detachment from social conventions. It's the case of NEETs, people who are Not in Employement, Education or Training for different reasons and are usually labeled as lazy or good for nothing.
Neetfix is a fictional brand of medicines, which is supposed to fix all the problems that afflict young people from this controvesial "Generation Z". The visual metaphor of a pill that can solves everything refers to the current approach of the institutions towards zoomers and, in particular, NEETs. Our reserch, however, pointed out that there are so many different cases and causes that a one-for-all solution is never going to work.
