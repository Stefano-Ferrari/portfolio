Our society has developed a deeper and deeper bond (which is almost an addiction) with the sense of sight. This predominance of the sight over other senses brought us to listen less, and unconsiously hear more.
Resonant is an independent magazine focused on how we "hear" the environment around us, how we relate with this soundscapes and how they affect us. The magazine contains stories, analysis and thoughts about our relationship with sound and noise but also visual experiments that "resonate" with the reader and its perception of sound.
Resonant explores five different fields of our society, starting with the #0 about displacement, which covers the sounds produced by people moving, emigrating and travelling.
