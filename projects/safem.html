During the pandemic we stopped travelling. The tourism was going through a crysis but, at the same time, people still felt the need to go back to travelling.
After a research about the changes in travel behaviours we designed SaFém, an app for mobile devices based on the concept of micro-tourism. The app aims to get people involved again in tourism, in a safer and more sustainable way.

We designed the wireframe and a full interactive prototipe of SaFém accessible on Figma.
