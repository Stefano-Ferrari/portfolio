Progetto 665 & the Nembro Files

BRANDING

STORYTELLING

Progetto 665 is the outcome of a co-design process made with three teenagers of Nembro, a town near Bergamo. The project propose a new way to experience the Museum of the coal mine, which actively involes the youth. The Nembro Files are a collection of findings and artifacts that together tell the story of the whole design process, thanks to visual metaphor.

labottegacremona

LaBottega is an exclusive restaurant located in the center of Cremona, Italy. The owner wanted to pay homage to the tradition of violin making, which the city is famous for.
Starting from the naming, I designed the brand identity for the restaurant using the typeface "New York", which was slightly edited for the logotype. The logo takes inspiration from the scroll of the violin and the textures from woodcraved decorations.

LaBottega is both a restaurant and a bar, so I used a color palette with two main colors, a dark teal for the restaurant and a bright orange for the bar.
